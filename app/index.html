THE BQI GRANTEE PORTAL

The BQI Grantee Portal will be turned on April 25th, 2014 alongside the launch of our first funding round.

At the moment, the portal supports the latest browsers, and is especially designed for Google Chrome and Firefox. We will be adding support for older browsers in the very near future.

For more information, please contact us at bigquestions@uchicago.edu.
